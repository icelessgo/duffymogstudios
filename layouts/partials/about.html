↳ a little bit about me

Twenty years behind a camera. Now I point it at love.

I started long before it was a career — falling in love with how a single image can hold an entire feeling. Over the years that obsession has taken me through fashion, events, portraits, and eventually to the thing that brings it all together: weddings.

Since becoming a parent, I think I understand weddings a little differently. These aren't just beautiful days — they're the start of something. Your photos become the story your family looks back on.

I'm based in Dublin, I travel everywhere in Ireland, and I promise — no awkward posing.
