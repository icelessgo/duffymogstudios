---
title: "FAQs | Duffymog Studios"
description: "Answers to the most common questions about booking Duffymog Studios for your wedding, elopement, or engagement photography in Dublin and across Ireland."
faq:
  - question: "What is your style of photography?"
    answer: "I try to achieve a best of both worlds with candids and documentary style throughout the day with some posed shots interspersed."
  - question: "Can we see a full gallery from a recent wedding?"
    answer: "Absolutely! Please get in touch and I would be happy to share a link to a gallery with permission from the bride and groom."
  - question: "Are pets welcome at shoots?"
    answer: "100% yes — bring them along! Pets are family and they make for the most joyful, candid moments. Whether it's your dog trotting down the aisle or joining you for engagement portraits, they are always welcome."
  - question: "Do you photograph civil ceremonies and non-religious weddings?"
    answer: "Absolutely — I photograph every kind of love. Whether you're getting married in a registry office, a farm, a beach, or a castle, I'm there for it."
  - question: "Do you offer a pre-wedding consultation?"
    answer: "Yes, and I love them! A pre-wedding chat is included with every package. It's a chance to talk through your timeline, must-have shots, any family dynamics to be aware of, and just get comfortable with each other before the big day."
  - question: "How do we book you for our wedding/engagement/portrait session?"
    answer: "A 25% deposit is required to lock in your date. This can be paid via Revolut, bank transfer, debit or credit card."
  - question: "Is there a fee for travel?"
    answer: "All wedding packages include travel anywhere in Ireland. Elopements and Engagements include travel within the Greater Dublin Area (Dublin, Kildare, Meath and Wicklow)."
  - question: "Are you insured?"
    answer: "Yes, covered up to €6.5m and cleared to work in venues such as the National Gallery of Ireland."
  - question: "How long does it typically take to receive the final photos?"
    answer: "Typically it takes four weeks but at peak times it can take up to six weeks. I promise they are worth the wait!"
  - question: "What format will we receive our photos in?"
    answer: "Your photos are delivered via a private online gallery where you can download the full-resolution files. You'll have full print rights — no watermarks, ever — so you can print, share, and treasure them however you like."
  - question: "How long will our gallery be available online?"
    answer: "Your private gallery will be available for 12 months after delivery. I'd recommend downloading everything shortly after you receive it, just to keep a copy safe on your own device."
  - question: "What happens if the weather is bad on our wedding day?"
    answer: "Ireland keeps us on our toes! I've shot in everything from glorious sunshine to horizontal rain, and honestly some of the most atmospheric and beautiful shots come from moody skies. I work with whatever the day brings and plan for indoor or sheltered alternatives when needed."
  - question: "Are there any additional fees or expenses I should be aware of?"
    answer: "If you are booking a wedding package I just kindly ask that you provide a meal for me (most venues will have this included as part of their packages)."
  - question: "What is your cancellation or postponement policy?"
    answer: "Life happens! If you need to postpone, I'll do my best to transfer your booking to a new date at no extra cost, subject to availability. In the event of a cancellation, the deposit is non-refundable as it secures your date exclusively for you. Please get in touch and we can work through it together."
